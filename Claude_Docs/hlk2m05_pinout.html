⚠ Your KiCad Symbol vs Physical Pins

KiCad Pin Label Physical Pin # Net Status

VO+ (pin 4 in symbol) Pin 3 +5V → needs named +5V symbol ⚠ Add +5V PWR symbol at wire top

⚡ ISOLATION WARNING: Pins 1 & 2 (AC side) are at mains voltage (90–264 V AC). There is 3000 V AC isolation between AC pins and DC pins. Never touch or probe AC-side pins while powered. The module GND (pin 4 / −Vo) is isolated from mains — this is safe for your ESP32-C6 circuit.
